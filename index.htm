Leo Skadden

Hello my name is Leo Skadden. I am currently a Computer Information Systems major at University of Wisconsin Stevens Point; a Student Technician for the Telephone Support Office as well as Network and Infrastructure department, both sub-departments of UWSP IT; the Treasurer for the UWSP Linux User Group; and a member of the UWSP Woodturning club. I grew up in St. Louis Park, Minnesota (a suburb of Minneapolis) and went to the local high school, St. Louis Park Senior High. During high school I played Alto Saxophone in the Wind Ensemble and Baritone Saxophone in the Jazz band; I current play my Alto recreationally.
